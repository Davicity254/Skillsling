**Gallery/Posts:**
- Photos and videos you upload

**Usage Data:**

- We do NOT collect your precise GPS location (we only use the location you manually enter)

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Where We Store Your Data

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) in the Europe multi-region (eur3) to ensure data redundancy and availability.

### 4.2 How We Protect Your Data

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:
- Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/SSL)
- Encryption of passwords
- Secure authentication systems
- Regular security assessments
- Access controls and authentication
- Firestore security rules

### 4.3 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You can request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us.

**Retention Periods:**
- Account data: Until you delete your account
- Messages: Until deleted by you or the recipient
- Posts/Gallery: Until you delete them
- Reviews: Retained even after account deletion (anonymized)
- Logs and analytics: Up to 90 days

### 4.4 Data Security Limitations

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.
